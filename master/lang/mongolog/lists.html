lists.pl -- List manipulation in mongolog programs.

The following predicates are supported:

PredicateArguments
length/2+List, ?Length
max_list/2+List, ?Max
min_list/2+List, ?Min
sum_list/2+List, ?Sum
member/2?Elem, +List
memberchk/2?Elem, +List
nth0/3?Index, +List, ?Elem
list_to_set/2+List, -Set
sort/2+List, -Sorted
